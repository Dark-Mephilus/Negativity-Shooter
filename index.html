📲 How to Install it on your Device

For Phone:
1. Tap the ⋮ (three dots) on your browser
2. Tap “Add to Home screen”
3. Confirm to install

For Desktop:
1. Click the install icon in the address bar if visible
OR
1. Click on the ⋮ (three dots) in the top right
2. Scroll down carefully
3. Click on (Cast/Save/Share) option and click “Install Negativity Shooting Game”
